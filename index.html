Our Story

More than a church —
a family

Living Friends Church has been a spiritual home in our Nepali community since 2016. We believe faith grows best in community — through honest conversations, generous service, and deep roots in Scripture.

🕊️

Friends of God

"I no longer call you servants… I have called you friends." — John 15:15. Our identity is rooted in this friendship with Christ.

🤝

Friends with Each Other

We build genuine community — across cultures, generations, and backgrounds — united by love and the Word.

🌏

Friends to the World

From Fort Worth to Nepal and beyond, we carry God's friendship to our neighborhoods and nations.
